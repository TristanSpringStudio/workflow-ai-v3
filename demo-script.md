# Vishtan Platform Demo Script
**Duration:** ~90 seconds
**Tone:** Confident, conversational, founder-led. Not salesy — show, don't tell.

---

## INTRO (10s)

"This is Vishtan. We interview your employees, map every workflow in your company, and show you exactly where AI fits in — and where it doesn't."

## HOME (10s)

*[Screen: Dashboard with "Good morning" greeting and chat input]*

"When you log in, you get a live summary of your organization. Top findings surface automatically — bottlenecks, single points of failure, quick wins. Click into any one to see the details, the departments involved, and the workflows affected."

## COMPANY INTELLIGENCE (15s)

*[Screen: Intelligence table with workflows listed]*

"Company Intelligence is every workflow we've extracted from your team's interviews. You can filter by department, by contributor, or by status — like bottlenecks or AI opportunities. Each row links to a deep dive."

## WORKFLOW DETAIL — MAP (20s)

*[Screen: Intelligence detail page, Map tab]*

"This is where it gets interesting. Every workflow is broken down step by step — who does what, what tools they use, where handoffs happen."

*[Toggle AI Recommendations On]*

"Toggle AI on, and you see the recommended future state. Pink steps are where AI takes over. The rest stay human. We're not replacing people — we're removing the busywork."

## AI IMPLEMENTATION TAB (10s)

*[Screen: AI Implementation tab]*

"Each workflow with an AI recommendation gets a full implementation guide — prerequisites, step-by-step rollout, success criteria, and a rollback plan. This isn't a slide deck. It's a playbook."

## IMPLEMENTATION PLAN (10s)

*[Screen: Roadmap page with 4 levels]*

"The implementation plan lays out your AI transformation in four levels — from quick wins you can ship this week, all the way to multi-agent systems next quarter. Every action item links back to a real workflow."

## AI ASSISTANT (10s)

*[Screen: AI Assistant chat]*

"And if you just want to ask a question — 'Where are our biggest bottlenecks?' or 'Who's a single point of failure in Engineering?' — the AI assistant has full context from every interview and every workflow we've mapped."

## CLOSE (5s)

"That's Vishtan. Interview your team, map the work, transform with AI."

---

**Total: ~90 seconds**

### Production Notes
- Screen record at 1920x1080
- Use Zippy Zaps demo company
- Pre-load a chat in the AI Assistant so streaming looks natural
- Toggle AI Recommendations live on camera for the "before/after" moment
- Keep mouse movements slow and deliberate
